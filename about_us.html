Provenance

Academic Roots.
Global Scale.

DEXYS was not born in a plush corporate boardroom. The vision took shape in 2025 while founders Dharsan and Naveenkumar were pursuing their engineering studies at VSB College of Engineering & Technical Campus, Coimbatore.

What began as an independent exploration into structural system limits—fueled by ambition, structured logic, and a frankly alarming amount of cafeteria coffee—quickly evolved into a fully realized technological enterprise.
