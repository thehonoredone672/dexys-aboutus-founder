Execution

Our Journey.

💡 2024

The Idea Stage

In 2024, the spark for DEXYS was ignited. A group of passionate tech enthusiasts began discussing the possibility of building a startup that could deliver smart, modern IT solutions. At that time, it was mostly brainstorming sessions, planning documents, and the classic startup ingredient — big dreams with bigger coffee cups.

🏗 2025

The Foundation

By 2025, those ideas started turning into reality. DEXYS officially established its organization and began offering its first set of services. This year was all about building the foundation — creating solutions, helping early clients, and learning something new every single day.

🚀 2026

Full Momentum

In 2026, DEXYS stepped into full momentum. We expanded our services, strengthened our expertise, and began building strong connections across the technology ecosystem. With growing experience and collaborations, we became more confident in delivering innovative and reliable IT solutions.
